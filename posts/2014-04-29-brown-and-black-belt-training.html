Brown and Black belt training

Posted on April 29, 2014 by Rik Hemsley

The 2nd of the black and brown belt sessions was lead by our own senior members.

Amelie took the students through key basics from Jion, whilst Angela concentrated on Kanku Dai.

Jane rounded off the session with Heian kata with key points and complete Enpi kata training.

Over 20 students turned out to support our 2nd brown and black belt training of the year.
